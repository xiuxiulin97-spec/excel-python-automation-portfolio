# Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 5, 2026

Excel Report Tool Desktop is a local desktop utility for working with Excel and CSV files.

## Data Collection

The app does not collect, store, sell, or transmit personal information to the developer or to a third-party service.

Files selected by the user are processed locally on the user's device. Generated reports are saved locally to locations chosen by the user.

## Internet Services

The app does not require an online account and does not upload user files for processing.

## User Control

Users control which local files are opened and where output reports are saved. Users can delete generated files from their own device at any time.
